Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Flowstate

What is Flowstate?▼
Flowstate is an AI-powered trading platform built around Inner Circle Trader (ICT) methodology. It identifies A+ setups using liquidity sweeps, fair value gaps, and market structure shifts — then enforces one-trade-per-day discipline to help traders pass and retain funded prop firm accounts.
How does the discipline lockout work?▼
Once your daily trade executes — whether it wins or loses — the platform locks your trading controls until the next session's London killzone analysis begins (02:00 EST). This prevents revenge trading, overtrading, and the emotional decisions that blow funded accounts.
Which instruments are supported?▼
Currently: NQ1! (Nasdaq futures), ES1! (S&P 500 futures), and GOLD (XAUUSD). Additional instruments including GBP/USD and crude oil are on the roadmap.
What is an ICT entry model?▼
An ICT entry model is a structured trade framework based on Smart Money Concepts. Each model requires: a liquidity sweep (BSL or SSL), an Inverted Fair Value Gap (IFVG) at the sweep level, a Change in State of Delivery (CISD), and a Market Structure Shift (BOS/MSS). All four must be present for an A+ signal.
Can I use this with my prop firm account?▼
Yes. Flowstate is built specifically for prop firm challenge accounts (FTMO, TopStep, Funded Trader, etc.). The 1% risk cap, one-trade-per-day limit, and discipline lockout are all aligned with standard prop firm rules. Always verify your specific firm's rules before enabling auto-execution.
What brokers are supported for auto-execution?▼
Tradovate (via TradingView webhook) is fully supported. Rithmic and OANDA (Gold) connections are in beta. We also support any broker accessible via TradingView's broker integration panel.
Is there a free trial?▼
Yes — all plans include a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. You'll get full access to your chosen plan's features during the trial period.
What is the refund policy?▼
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee from your first payment. If you're not satisfied, contact support within 14 days for a full refund. After 14 days, charges are non-refundable.
